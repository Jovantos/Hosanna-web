Admissions

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Medical examination report
  • Transfer letter (if applicable)

Enrollment Procedures

  1. Submit completed application form with required documents
  2. Attend an interview with parents/guardians
  3. Complete entrance examination
  4. Receive admission letter if successful
  5. Pay required fees
  6. Complete registration process

Fee Structure

Item Amount
Tuition Fee (per term) $500

Library Fee (annual) $50
