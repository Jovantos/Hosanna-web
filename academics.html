Academic Programs

Our Curriculum

At Hosanna Secondary School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for both national examinations and future academic pursuits. Our curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills.

Academic Departments

Mathematics Department

Our mathematics program focuses on developing strong analytical and problem-solving skills through practical applications and theoretical understanding.

Science Department

Students engage in hands-on laboratory work and research projects, covering Biology, Chemistry, and Physics.

Languages Department

We offer comprehensive language programs in English, Literature, and other modern languages to enhance communication skills.

Humanities Department

Our humanities program includes History, Geography, and Social Studies, fostering global awareness and cultural understanding.
